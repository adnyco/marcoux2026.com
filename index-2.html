About Aaron

Aaron Marcoux is the Washburn County District Attorney, a role he has held since 2019 and won by election in 2020 and 2024.

He brings experience across the justice system, beginning his legal career with the Wisconsin State Public Defender’s Office in Ashland, serving Northern Wisconsin communities from 2010–2015.

He later served as an Assistant District Attorney in Sawyer County (2015–2019) before returning to serve Washburn County.

A Rice Lake High School graduate, Aaron earned degrees from UW–Eau Claire Barron County and UW–Milwaukee, and graduated from the University of Wyoming College of Law.

In 2024, he was named UW–Eau Claire Barron County’s Distinguished Alumnus.

Aaron lives in Washburn County with his wife, Elizabeth, and their six children and coaches school athletics locally.
