About Aaron

Aaron Marcoux has served Washburn County as District Attorney since 2019. He has worked across the justice system as a prosecutor and as a Wisconsin Assistant State Public Defender.

He lives in Washburn County with his wife and six children and coaches high school basketball.

Priorities

Experience. Fairness. Respect.

  • Prepared and even-tempered in the courtroom
  • Fair and impartial… committed to applying the law
  • Efficient case management to help the court run smoothly and resolve matters in a timely way
  • Respect for every person who comes before the court
